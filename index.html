The Process

From setup to
signed offer

01
Set Up Your Session
Enter your name, upload your PDF résumé, and let the platform extract your skills and project history automatically.
02
Enter the Video Interview
Join the live video session. All three AI agents are present. One speaks at a time, and 30% of the time another agent jumps in with a follow-up.
03
Code in the Live Workspace
Switch to the coding challenge room. Monaco editor, resizable panels, code execution, test runner, and AI-powered code review — all in one place.
04
Read Your Interview Report
Get a full breakdown: per-question scores (1–10), preferred answers, strengths, weaknesses, and a downloadable HTML report.
